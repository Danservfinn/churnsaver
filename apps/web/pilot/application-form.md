## Terms & Conditions

### Pilot Agreement

By applying for the Churn Saver Pilot Program, you agree to:

1. **Data Sharing**: Share anonymized performance metrics for program improvement
2. **Feedback**: Provide regular feedback through surveys and calls
3. **Testing**: Thoroughly test features and report bugs
4. **Promotion**: Allow use of your results in case studies (with permission)
5. **Engagement**: Participate actively in weekly check-ins and community

### Confidentiality

- Your specific revenue numbers will remain private
- Individual results will not be shared without permission
- Program learnings will be anonymized

### Termination

- Either party can terminate with 7 days notice
- Data export available upon request
- Pro-rated refunds for paid features

### Liability

- Pilot software provided "as is"
- No guaranteed results or uptime
- Standard terms apply for data handling
