# Churn Saver - Automatic Payment Recovery

## App Title
**Churn Saver** - Smart Payment Recovery & Revenue Protection

## Tagline
Automatically recover failed payments and reduce churn with intelligent nudges and incentives.

## Description

### The Problem
Payment failures are costing creators thousands in lost revenue. When subscriptions fail, most users never come back - they just move on to competitors. Manual recovery is time-consuming and ineffective.

### The Solution
Churn Saver automatically detects failed payments, sends personalized recovery messages, and applies incentives to win back subscribers. Our intelligent system learns from successful recoveries to maximize your revenue recovery rate.
