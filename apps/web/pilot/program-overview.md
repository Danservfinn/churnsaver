# Churn Saver Pilot Program

## Overview

The Churn Saver Pilot Program is designed to validate product-market fit, gather user feedback, and demonstrate revenue impact before full market launch. This structured program ensures we launch with proven results and enthusiastic early adopters.

## Program Goals

### Primary Objectives
1. **Validate Recovery Performance**: Achieve ≥10% recovery rates across pilot participants (PRD requirement: T-020)
2. **Gather User Feedback**: Collect detailed insights on usability and feature gaps
3. **Demonstrate ROI**: Show measurable revenue impact for pilot creators
4. **Build Testimonials**: Create compelling case studies and social proof
5. **Refine Product**: Identify and implement critical improvements

### Success Criteria
- **Recovery Rate**: ≥10% average across all pilots (PRD requirement: T-020)
- **User Satisfaction**: >4.5/5 NPS score
- **Revenue Impact**: >$500/month average additional revenue
- **Retention**: >90% pilot continuation rate
- **Referrals**: >30% referral rate to full launch

## Pilot Structure

### Phase 1: Setup & Training (Week 1)

**Activities:**
- Creator onboarding and training
- Initial configuration and testing
- Baseline metrics establishment
- Technical support setup

**Deliverables:**
- Configured Churn Saver installation
- Baseline payment failure data
- Creator feedback on setup process

### Phase 2: Optimization (Weeks 2-3)

**Activities:**
- Performance monitoring and analysis
- A/B testing of different strategies
- Configuration optimization
- Weekly check-ins and support

**Deliverables:**
- Optimized recovery strategies
- Performance improvement metrics
- Creator optimization insights

### Phase 3: Scale & Validation (Week 4)

**Activities:**
- Full rollout to all memberships
- Extended performance monitoring
- Comprehensive feedback collection
- Case study preparation

**Deliverables:**
- Full-scale performance metrics
- Creator testimonials and case studies
- Product improvement recommendations

## Participant Selection

### Target Profile
- **Revenue**: $1,000+/month recurring revenue
- **Subscribers**: 50+ active subscribers
- **Failure Rate**: 5%+ monthly payment failure rate
- **Engagement**: Active in creator communities
- **Technical**: Comfortable with basic configuration

### Selection Process
1. **Application**: Interested creators apply via form
2. **Qualification**: Revenue and subscriber verification
3. **Interview**: 15-minute call to assess fit
4. **Onboarding**: Technical requirements and expectations
5. **Activation**: Account setup and training

### Cohort Size
- **Phase 1**: 10 creators (diverse niches)
- **Phase 2**: 25 creators (scaled validation)
- **Phase 3**: 50 creators (market validation)

## Pilot Metrics

### Primary Metrics

#### Recovery Performance
- **Recovery Rate**: (Successful Recoveries / Total Cases) × 100
- **Revenue Recovered**: Total $ from attributed recoveries
- **Recovery Velocity**: Average time to recovery
- **Case Volume**: Total recovery cases processed

#### User Experience
- **Setup Time**: Time to complete initial configuration
- **Dashboard Usage**: Feature adoption and engagement
- **Support Tickets**: Issues encountered and resolution time
- **Feature Requests**: New functionality suggestions

### Secondary Metrics

#### Business Impact
- **ROI**: Revenue recovered vs incentive costs
- **Subscriber Retention**: Impact on churn rates
- **Revenue Growth**: Overall revenue trend during pilot
- **Cost Efficiency**: Cost per recovered subscriber

#### Product Health
- **System Uptime**: Application availability
- **Webhook Success**: Webhook delivery and processing
- **Performance**: Dashboard load times and responsiveness
- **Error Rates**: Application and webhook errors

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **System Downtime**: Redundant infrastructure and monitoring
- **Data Loss**: Automated backups and recovery procedures
- **Integration Issues**: Comprehensive testing and rollback plans
- **Performance Problems**: Load testing and scaling procedures

### User Experience Risks
- **Setup Difficulties**: Simplified onboarding and support
- **Poor Performance**: Clear expectations and success criteria
- **Data Privacy**: Transparent policies and opt-out procedures
- **Support Overload**: Scaled support team and automation

### Business Risks
- **Low Adoption**: Rigorous selection and engagement
- **Poor Results**: Clear success criteria and optimization support
- **Negative Feedback**: Proactive communication and improvement
- **Competition**: First-mover advantage and unique positioning
